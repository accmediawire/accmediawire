PPC ads are hyper-targeted

PPC is considered as one of the most hyper-targeted marketing strategies, although many tactics can let you target and reach your potential customers and PPC stands on number 1 in terms of hyper-targeted ads.

Options to target your audience with Google ads:

Demographics: Demographic targeting with your PPC campaigns will let you reach your target audience based on their location, gender, and the device type they are going to use.

In-market: This is one of the most effective and good ways to target your audience who are searching for SEO services or products that your store offers too. 

Custom intent: Custom intent lets you choose words, keywords, and phrases that relate to all the users who can certainly purchase on your site. 

Remarketing: Remarketing allows you to serve your ads to all the people who did click on your ads earlier. Remarketing is also beneficial in brand awareness and it keeps your ads and services at the top of your potential customer's minds and it gives results too.

Similar audiences: This feature is one step ahead in the digital marketing world and will be converting more for your business. It works on your remarketing list data and it expands the reach of your audiences and targets new users, with similar behavior and characteristics.

Content keywords: This feature allows you to target the users with specific keywords who search for the same keyword. It also helps to target a specific demographic or search intent and if done properly it delivers great results.
